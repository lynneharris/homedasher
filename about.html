Our Story

Built by a parent
in survival mode

HomeDasher was founded by a parent in survival mode.

Three kids, three dogs, and two working parents trying to keep up. Like a lot of families, we couldn't find help that actually fit our life. Cleaning services work from a standard checklist. Our life didn't — every day was a different to-do list.

What we needed was a way to quickly offload the actual work — not just think about it, but hand it off and have it done. The HomeDasher Planner builds your custom to-do list in minutes: tell it what's piling up today, and your HomeDasher shows up already knowing exactly what to tackle. No explanation, no orientation. Just results.

How we hire and train

Every HomeDasher goes through a careful hiring process before they ever step foot in a client's home. That includes a thorough background check, hands-on training, and an apprenticeship working alongside experienced HomeDasher managers — building the skills and confidence to handle every type of task independently.

We're looking for people who are resourceful, warm, and genuinely take pride in their work. Most of our HomeDashers have backgrounds as household managers, housekeepers, or experienced parents — people who understand what it means to keep a home running well. We compensate them fairly as W-2 employees, because great service starts with a team that feels valued.
