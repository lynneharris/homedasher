About HomeDasher

HomeDasher is an on-demand household help service serving the Greater Seattle Area. We send a skilled HomeDasher to your home — someone who can handle cleaning, laundry, meal prep, home admin, pet and plant care, and virtually anything else on your household to-do list.

What makes us different is the HomeDasher Planner — our proprietary task intake system. Before your appointment, you tell the Planner what's piling up today. It builds a fully custom to-do list in minutes and delivers it directly to your HomeDasher before they arrive. They show up already knowing what to tackle, where things are, and how you like things done.

No orientation. No explaining. Working from your to-do list, not ours.

HomeDasher currently serves the Greater Seattle Area. Not sure if we cover your neighborhood? Start a booking and enter your address — we'll let you know right away.

HomeDashers are highly trained, fairly compensated W-2 employees — not gig workers. They're skilled helpers capable of jumping into every role needed to keep a household running smoothly, from cleaning and laundry to meal prep, virtual assistant tasks, and everything in between. Think of your HomeDasher as a second set of hands — someone who can clone the capable, organized version of you.

Most of our HomeDashers have extensive backgrounds as household managers, housekeepers, cleaning professionals, or experienced parents who've spent years keeping their own households running at an efficient clip. They're comfortable using their own devices to research and handle tasks quickly, managing complex to-do lists, and adapting to whatever your household needs that day.

Because HomeDashers are W-2 employees, they're fully insured, background-checked, and accountable to HomeDasher's quality standards — not just passing through on their way to the next gig.

All HomeDashers are W-2 employees — never 1099 contractors. This means they're covered by HomeDasher's insurance, held to our quality and training standards, and treated as real members of our team. It's better for them, and better for you.

Services

HomeDashers can handle a wide range of household tasks — not just cleaning. Our standard services include:

  • Cleaning — surfaces, mirrors, toilets, dishes, fridge cleanout, trash, sweeping and vacuuming
  • Laundry & Linens — wash, dry, fold, put away, change beds, organize linen closet
  • Meal Prep & Planning — grocery lists, ingredient prep, simple cooking from your recipes, pantry organization
  • Tidying & Organizing — any room, drawers, closets, decluttering, seasonal swaps, event prep, unpacking
  • Home Admin — phone calls, research, scheduling, emails, form completion, digital organization, mail sorting
  • Pet & Plant Care — feeding, water, medication, litter box, plant watering and maintenance

Need something not on the list? Tell the HomeDasher Planner — chances are we can help.

Each HomeDasher arrives with a small professional cleaning kit including:

  • Multi-purpose cleaning and sanitizing spray
  • Window and mirror cleaner
  • Stainless steel cleaner
  • Paper towels
  • Disposable toilet wands

For everything else — vacuum, broom, additional cleaning products — your HomeDasher uses what's already in your home. For research, scheduling, and admin tasks, HomeDashers use their own devices. Your computer is never accessed. WiFi access is always appreciated to make sure admin tasks are completed quickly.

💡 We especially love helping families going through overwhelming seasons — new babies, a household member recovering from illness, or just a period where the list has gotten longer than the hours in the day. HomeDashers are always happy to wear shoe covers, gloves, and masks (N95+ protection) on request.

Some tasks — like deep bathroom or kitchen cleans, carpet cleaning, junk hauling, pressure washing, and move-out deep cleans — require a specialist HomeDasher with the right equipment and products for your specific home.

When you use the HomeDasher Planner, simply mention what you need. We'll note your specialty request and someone will reach out to schedule it separately — so your specialist arrives already knowing about your home's surfaces and can bring exactly the right products. Your regular booking continues without interruption.

HomeDashers are happy to help with pet feeding, water, medication, litter box, and pet area cleanup. Just include it in your to-do list when booking.

Because we care about the safety of both your pets and our team, rabies vaccination records are required on file for pet care. After you book, we'll send you a separate link to upload your pet's records — it won't hold up your booking today.

Booking & Pricing

Booking takes just a few minutes. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Use the HomeDasher Planner to build your custom to-do list — tell us what needs doing today
  • Step 2: Enter your details, choose a date and time from real-time availability, and select how many hours you need
  • Step 3: Pay securely — your HomeDasher is assigned and arrives with your complete to-do list already in hand

Start building your to-do list →

HomeDasher charges a simple flat rate of $50/hour with a 1-hour minimum. That's it — no hidden fees, no add-ons, no surprises. The HomeDasher Planner and your custom to-do list are always included.

Cancel any time up to one hour before your appointment for a full refund, no questions asked. We know life happens.

Same-day bookings are welcome — all you need is at least one hour's notice. Availability is shown in real time when you book, so what you see is what's available.

Safety & Trust

HomeDasher is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Every HomeDasher goes through a careful hiring process that includes a thorough background check, hands-on training, and an apprenticeship working alongside experienced HomeDasher managers before their first independent visit.

We hold our team to a high standard — so problems are rare. But if something doesn't meet your expectations, we want to know. Reach out and we'll make it right.

We take the health of your household seriously. Here's how we protect it:

  • HomeDashers bring their own professional cleaning kit — no cross-contamination from other homes
  • Our multi-purpose spray uses hypochlorous acid — a hospital-grade sanitizer that's completely safe for babies, pets, and sensitive surfaces
  • HomeDashers wear shoe covers, gloves, and masks (N95+ protection) on request — just let us know when booking
  • HomeDashers stay home when sick — we'll arrange a substitute or reschedule at no penalty to you

We especially love supporting families with newborns and households managing illness or recovery. Don't hesitate to let us know your needs when booking.
